Ukraine Restored

As many people already know, Russia is in an ongoing invasion of Ukraine since Febuary of 2022. Russia isn't holding back even when it comes to humanity launching missiles at citizen faucilities and The Russia-Ukraine crisis has been ongoing since 2014, but it has escalated since the invasion of Ukraine in February of 2022. According to Reuters.com, a worldwide news network, there have been 29,916 recorded deaths in the war so far. There has been $350 billion in property damage, as well as 53,616 injuries. 14 million people have been displaced from their homes. According to the State Road Agency of Ukraine, more than 24,000 km of destroyed road.

Though Ukraine is fighting off Russia from many of its cities, its economy is in danger and will continue to be even after the war ends. With buildings and banks destroyed, fields and livestock gone, Ukraine will need some help to get back on its feet to be stable again and help provide to the world. The Ukrainian Humanitarian Aid Commision (UHAC) is dedicated to providing humanitarian aid for the people and the country of Ukraine. Our three-phase plan consists of providing immediate aid for people in need by providing food and medicine, aiding reconstruction, and providing long term aid by rebuilding infrastructure in the country. We have 10 billion dollars to work with, and we have planned out our expenses so that no money will be wasted.
