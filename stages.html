Stages of Rebuilding

Our plan divides reconstruction into three phases that focus on both the short term and long term. The first phase focuses on immediate needs and supplies like medicine that will help to maintain Ukrainian infrastructure.

The second phase will take place after the end of the war and will focus on physically rebuilding the country with emphasis placed on transportation, shelter, and getting refugees back into the country.

The final phase will focus on supporting future generations though healthcare, education, and employment.
